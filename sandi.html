Logika Program:

Sensor LDR:

  • Nilai 0-50: "keadaan gelap" ‚Üí LED MATI
  • Nilai 51-500: "keadaan redup" ‚Üí LED NYALA üí°

  • Sumbu X < -10: "sensor miring kanan"
  • Sumbu Y > 10: "sensor miring depan"
  • Sumbu Y < -10: "sensor miring belakang"
